Why Choose Us

Huge School Campus

The diversification and expansion of the School through adoption of new and tropically relevant courses has led furthur enhancement of our infrastructure in order to fulfill our growing needs.

Computer Education

Computer have taken over a major part of human activities and enabled people to have more leisure by reducing his work-load.Computer Education is started from the primary classes and is one of the optional subjects is higher grades.

Music and Dance

Training of Vocal as well as instrumental music is provided according to the taste and intrest of the students from Std. I to XII.'Indian classical' and'Western dance' are taught in the school by experienced teacher to help,train and guide the students to be intrested in this for of art.

Admission Procedure

Please visit us with prior appointment between 7:20 am to 1:30 pm any day from Monday to Friday and on Saturday between 8:00 am to 12:00 pm. The admission office will give detailed information about the school,methodology,facilities,fee structure and guide you with the admission procedure followed by the school tour.

Lab Facilities
